Spiderman of Queens, New York

Introducing Spiderman, your friendly neighborhood spider that protects the city of New York.

Spiderman is the best superhero of Queens, New York! He continaully ensures that the city is safe and that any criminal is justified for their crimes. Like more superheroes, he has a great sense and can react to objects coming towards his way without sight. Additionally, he has web shooters at his wrists which he obtained after a radioactive spider bit him at a lab. Now, he swings across the city and protects the state of New York!
